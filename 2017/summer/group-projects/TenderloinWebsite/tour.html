Tour

This tour will take you through the tenderloin and expose you to the conditions of the neighborhood. This can be an opportunity for you to see what some people have to go through on their everyday life that you may not have proper knowledge about. Hopefully by the end of the tour you see the problems the community has and it inspires you to not avoid visiting the neighborhood.



Key locations to visit:

  • Saint Boniface Catholic Church

  • St. Anthony's Dining hall

  • St Anthony Foundation Tech Lab

  • Tenderloin Museum

  • Cadillac

  • And many others

    • To learn more about these places visit our 'Places and Figures' page
